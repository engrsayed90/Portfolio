About me

I'm Md Saydur Rahman and I'm Robotics Additive Manufacturing Engineer

I am an automation and robotics engineer with hands-on experience in 3D printing technology, robot control, and digital manufacturing. Currently working at Nippon Hume Co., Ltd. in the Technology Development Department, I specialize in mortar-based 3D printing using Yaskawa YRC1000 robots. I design with Rhino and Grasshopper, automate and monitor production through AiSync by Ai Build, and simulate operations using MotosimEG-VRC. Previously, I worked as an Automation Engineer (SMT & AOI) at Sanshin Electric Co., Ltd., where I developed and debugged data for surface-mount and optical inspection systems, enhancing production quality and efficiency. I am passionate about using automation, digital design, and robotics to drive innovation in the future of manufacturing.
